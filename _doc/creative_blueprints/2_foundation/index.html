Why Nurseries Need a Better System

✕

The Problem

  • Missed care = dead plants

    Without a schedule, watering and treatment windows are forgotten — causing preventable plant loss that directly reduces saleable inventory.

  • Stock-demand mismatch

    Nurseries over-stock slow movers and under-stock spring bestsellers — wasting money and missing peak revenue windows every season.

  • Disconnected records

    Care logs, inventory counts, and sales data live in notebooks and spreadsheets — making confident purchasing decisions nearly impossible.

✓

The Solution

NurseryFlow gives nursery owners a single dashboard that connects plant care, inventory, and sales data — so every plant stays healthy and every seasonal peak is fully stocked.

🌱 Real-time inventory

Every plant tracked by species, quantity, and health status.

💧 Smart care schedules

Automated reminders for watering, feeding, and treatment.

📊 Seasonal demand view

Historical sales patterns guide stocking decisions for each season.

🔔 Health alerts

Catch overdue care before plants are lost.
