# NurseryFlow — Product Overview

## What It Is

NurseryFlow is a plant nursery management app purpose-built for nursery owners and
operators. It consolidates three critical operational areas — plant inventory, care
scheduling, and seasonal sales — into a single focused workspace. The core promise:
keep plants healthy and match stock levels to seasonal demand.

## Target Users

**Primary persona: Independent nursery owner / small nursery operator**

- Manages dozens to hundreds of plant varieties simultaneously
- Responsible for both plant health care and business sales performance
- Currently relies on manual logs, spreadsheets, or memory for tracking
- Peak seasons (spring, summer) create high-pressure demand spikes that are difficult
  to anticipate and stock for

The same person often wears multiple hats — plant care lead, stock manager, and
sales planner — all in one day.

## Core Problems Solved

1. **Plant health failures from missed care**: Without a systematic schedule, plants
   miss watering and treatment windows — causing preventable losses that directly
   reduce saleable inventory.
2. **Stock-demand mismatch**: Nurseries routinely over-stock slow-moving varieties and
   under-stock fast sellers around seasonal peaks, resulting in waste and missed revenue.
3. **Disconnected records**: Care logs, inventory counts, and sales data live in
   notebooks and spreadsheets, making confident purchasing or propagation decisions
   nearly impossible.

## Product Scope

NurseryFlow handles:

- **Plant inventory tracking** — Track every plant by species, variety, quantity,
  location, and health status in real time.
- **Watering & care scheduling** — Create recurring care routines by plant type;
  receive reminders when watering, fertilizing, or treatment is due.
- **Seasonal sales tracking** — Log and review sales by plant, season, and time period
  to understand what sells when.
- **Stock-demand matching** — Use historical sales patterns to inform purchasing and
  propagation plans aligned to seasonal demand peaks.
- **Health & overdue alerts** — Surface plants that are overdue for care so issues are
  caught before they become losses.
- **Inventory reports** — Generate summaries that drive purchasing decisions, waste
  reduction, and seasonal planning reviews.

## Strategic Principles

- **Plant health first**: The core promise is a healthier, more sellable inventory —
  not just digital paperwork.
- **Seasonal intelligence**: The product's differentiator is connecting care data and
  inventory data to sales seasonality — treating the nursery as a living, seasonal
  business.
- **Simplicity for operators**: Nursery owners are not tech-heavy users. The UI must be
  fast to learn, low-friction to use daily, and comfortable on the greenhouse floor.
- **Actionable visibility**: Every screen should help the owner make a decision — water
  today, order more of this variety, reduce that SKU next spring.

## Key Outcomes

- Fewer plant losses through proactive, scheduled care
- Better sell-through rates at seasonal demand peaks
- Reduced overstock and end-of-season waste
- Time saved replacing manual logs with automated scheduling
- Confident purchasing decisions grounded in historical sales data

## Brand & Tone

- **Product name**: NurseryFlow
- **Tagline**: Keep every plant thriving, every season.
- **Tone**: Calm, grounded, practical. Speaks like a knowledgeable colleague, not a
  tech platform. Warm but businesslike.
- **Color palette**: Emerald greens as primary (growth, health, nature), warm slate
  neutrals for text and structure, clean white space.
- **Visual language**: Natural, editorial, premium. Photography-forward. No cutesy
  plant clip-art.
